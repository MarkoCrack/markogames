The end

Today I'm updating PH for the last time and archiving the project for several reasons. Unfortunately it's not something that I enjoy working on anymore. It was exciting to develop it was just gaining traction, but eventually the high wore off and I was left to cope with the fact that my most popular work was something that I wasn't proud of, technically or morally. The site was something that I put together in a few hours, and I hadn't expected it to be something I'd have to maintain, and I struggled to justify working on a games site that enabled procrastination and some bad choices.
As I approach a turning point in my life I want to focus on doing more good, and PLAYHYDRA just doesn't fit that vision.
So, the site will stay up indefinitely but don't be surprised it it falls to disrepair.
Thank you so much for all the emails (I apologize to everyone who I couldn't respond to), for the support, and for proving to me that I can create things that people care about.

It's been a pleasure,
Liam
